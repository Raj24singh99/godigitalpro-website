# Agency Partner Program

Documentation and resources for the GoDigitalPro Agency Partner Program.

## Program Overview

Three partnership models:

| Model | How It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| **Resell Systems** | License our frameworks under your brand. You set price, we provide infrastructure. |
| **Project Collaboration** | Co-manage client projects. Split workload, share revenue. |
| **Full White-Label** | We execute everything under your brand. You keep the client relationship. |

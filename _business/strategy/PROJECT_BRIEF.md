## Phase 1: Foundation & Validation (Months 1–3)
**Timeline:** April – June 2026

### Goals
- Position GoDigitalPro as AI Marketing Agency (broad, not EdTech-only)
- Acquire 2–3 paying clients and run them through the full system
- Build internal workflows, automation, and SOPs
- Document everything for later productization
- Create case studies and proof of concept

| Social Media Marketing | Organic + paid social |
| Creative & Video Production | High-velocity creative testing |

## Phase 2: Scale & Productize (Months 4–6)
**Timeline:** July – October 2026

### Goals
- Package proven systems as Whop products
- Launch Agency Partner program
- Refine workflows from Phase 1 learnings
- Generate revenue from both agency services + Whop products

### Whop Products (Planned)
1. White-label marketing system templates
2. Agency Partner Program (resell / co-manage / white-label)
3. Individual tools / mini-courses

### Agency Partner Models
| Model | Structure |
|-------|-----------|
| Resell Systems | White-label frameworks + training + margins |
| Project Collaboration | Co-manage projects + revenue share |
| Full White-Label | We execute, they bill the client |

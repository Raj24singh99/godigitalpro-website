# Source Playbook: Directories

## Targets
- Clutch/GoodFirms/industry directories
- Niche-specific edtech and proptech listings

## Why This Works
- Businesses are actively presenting services
- Easier to segment by category + location

## Data Capture
- Company
- Website
- Niche
- Decision-maker (if available)
- Email/contact form URL

## Caution
- Avoid generic blast lists
- Prioritize leads with clear service-market fit

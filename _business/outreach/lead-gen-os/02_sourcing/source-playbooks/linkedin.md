# Source Playbook: LinkedIn

## Use Cases
- Founder-led businesses
- B2B and EdTech decision makers

## Filters
- Industry
- Headcount
- Geography
- Role keywords (Founder, Growth, Marketing Head)

## Data Capture
- Company name
- Website
- Decision-maker name
- Role
- LinkedIn URL
- Public business email (if available)

## Quality Flags
- Posted recently
- Hiring for sales/marketing
- Running active campaigns/content

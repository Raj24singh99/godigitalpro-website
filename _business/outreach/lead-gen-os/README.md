# Lead Gen OS (GoDigitalPro)

This workspace is your operating system for lead generation across core niches:
- EdTech
- Real Estate
- B2B Services

## Folder Map

- `00_strategy`: positioning, offer strategy, lead-finding priority
- `01_niches`: ICP + pain points + messaging per niche
- `02_sourcing`: where and how to find qualified leads
- `03_qualification_scoring`: lead scoring model and qualification rules
- `04_outreach`: prompts, email frameworks, and follow-up templates
- `05_campaign_execution`: live campaign trackers per niche
- `06_automation`: workflow roadmap (Apps Script, scoring, enrichment, PPT)
- `07_reporting`: KPIs and dashboards
- `08_ops`: weekly review and experiment process

## Suggested Weekly Rhythm

1. Monday: source + qualify leads
2. Tuesday: send first-touch campaigns
3. Wednesday: follow-ups + optimize copy
4. Thursday: deep audits for high-intent prospects
5. Friday: KPI review + experiment planning

# ICP: EdTech

## Target Segments
- Online course businesses
- Test prep/coaching institutes
- Upskilling platforms

## Buyer Personas
- Founder/Co-founder
- Growth/Marketing Head
- Admissions/Enrollment Lead

## Pain Points
- High CPL from paid channels
- Low enrollment conversion rate
- Weak lead qualification pipeline
- Slow or cluttered landing pages

## Qualification Filters
- Active website + clear offer
- Has lead capture process (form/call/WhatsApp)
- Revenue stage: stable operations, wants scale

## Outreach Angle
"Increase qualified enrollments with better funnel conversion + lead quality systems."

# Lead Score Model

## Purpose
Prioritize outreach to leads most likely to convert.

## Score Components (100 total)
- Niche fit (0-20)
- Buyer persona fit (0-20)
- Website quality gap opportunity (0-20)
- Signs of active growth intent (0-20)
- Contactability data quality (0-20)

## Suggested Thresholds
- 70+ = High Priority (outreach immediately)
- 50-69 = Medium Priority (enrich more, then outreach)
- <50 = Low Priority (hold)

## Example Qualitative Signals
- Has ad intent but weak funnel
- Poor speed/UX and weak CTA flow
- No clear lead handling process

# Lead Finding Priority (By Niche)

## EdTech
1. LinkedIn (founders, growth leads, admissions heads)
2. Company websites (school/edtech platforms with weak funnels)
3. Product directories + startup databases

## Real Estate
1. Google Maps (agencies, brokers, developers)
2. Local property portals + broker directories
3. LinkedIn (brokerage owners, marketing heads)

## B2B Services
1. LinkedIn (agency founders, consultants, operations heads)
2. Clutch/GoodFirms sort of directories
3. Company websites with weak conversion UX

## High-Intent Signal Checklist
- Running ads but weak landing pages
- Old website + slow speed
- Low conversion CTAs
- Weak trust signals (case studies/reviews)
- No visible lead magnet or booking funnel

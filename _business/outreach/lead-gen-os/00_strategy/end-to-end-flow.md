# End-to-End Flow (Lead Gen to Close)

## Stage 1: Define Niche + ICP
- Pick one niche for each sprint
- Confirm buyer roles and qualification rules

## Stage 2: Source Leads
- Pull 100 raw leads from 2-3 channels
- Fill mandatory CRM fields

## Stage 3: Qualify + Score
- Apply score model
- Keep only high-priority leads for outreach

## Stage 4: Personalized Outreach
- Run analysis-based email automation
- Keep CTA single and clear (audit call)

## Stage 5: Follow-up and Reply Handling
- Follow-up 1, 2, 3 with value-add
- Update reply stage in CRM sheet

## Stage 6: Sales Handoff
- Book discovery call
- Prepare custom mini-audit/PPT
- Move to proposal stage

## Stage 7: Optimization Loop
- Review KPI weekly
- Improve hooks, targeting, and scoring rules
- Scale top-performing niche/channel pairs

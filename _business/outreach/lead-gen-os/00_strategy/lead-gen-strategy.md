# Lead Generation Strategy

## Goal
Build a predictable pipeline of qualified discovery calls for GoDigitalPro.

## Core Model
1. Niche-specific ICP
2. High-intent lead sourcing
3. Qualification + scoring
4. Personalized outreach
5. Follow-up system
6. Conversion tracking + optimization

## Offer Positioning (Lead-Gen Focus)
- Primary offer: lead generation system setup + optimization
- Entry offer: free/low-friction 15-minute growth audit
- Outcome promise: better lead quality, faster conversion, lower CAC waste

## Channel Mix (Recommended Start)
- Cold email (primary)
- LinkedIn outbound (secondary)
- Retargeting for warm responders (later phase)

## Quality Rules
- Do not outreach unqualified leads
- Use niche-specific pain points only
- Keep first email short and outcome-focused
- Track every stage in CRM sheet

## 30-Day Execution Plan
1. Week 1: finalize ICP + scoring + templates
2. Week 2: source 150 leads (50 per niche)
3. Week 3: launch outreach + follow-up 1
4. Week 4: optimize based on reply and meeting rate
